Who am I?

Ugur Dogrusoy


I'm Ugur Dogrusoy, and I currently reside in Istanbul.
I graduated from Mersin University with a degree in Computer Technologies and Programming.
I specialize in UI/UX Design and also work as a Web Front-End Developer. I have been in this field for 9 years.
I actively follow the latest developments in the design field on foreign websites such as Dribbble, Behance, DesignerNews, Smashing Magazine, Codrops, Speckyboy, and Medium. I have experience with project management methodologies like Agile, Scrum, and others. My mission is to create use cases based on user experience principles, resulting in contemporary design elements. I aim to blend design trends, color, typography, and user experiences to produce effective products while paying attention to all the details of design architecture. In web-based projects, I develop responsive and mobile designs with a maintainable architecture, using JavaScript in addition to web front-end development.
